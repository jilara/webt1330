SCP-049

SCP-049, also referred to as the Plague Doctor, is a major character in the SCP Foundation series. He is a Euclid-class object under the Foundation's containment. He is a humanoid figure that resembles a 15-16th century European Plague Doctor, with a black robe and a white-beaked mask. He is obsessed with curing every person he encounters of a mysterious "pestilence" only he is aware of, by turning those people into zombies.
