Canon EOS R5

The Canon EOS R5 is a high-end mirrorless camera that is perfect for professional photographers and videographers. It has a full-frame 45MP CMOS sensor that can capture stunning images and videos in even the most challenging lighting conditions. It can also record 8K video at up to 30fps and 4K video at up to 120fps.

Full-frame mirrorless camera 45MP CMOS sensor 8K video recording ISO 100-51200 20fps continuous shooting 5-axis in-body image stabilization Electronic viewfinder Vari-angle touchscreen LCD Dual Pixel CMOS AF II Wi-Fi and Bluetooth connectivity
Canon EOS R6

The Canon EOS R6 is a great choice for enthusiast and semi-professional photographers. It has a full-frame 20MP CMOS sensor that can capture high-quality images in a variety of lighting conditions. It can also record 4K video at up to 60fps.

Canon EOS R7

The Canon EOS R7 is a great choice for sports and wildlife photographers. It has an APS-C 32.5MP CMOS sensor that can capture high-resolution images and videos of fast-moving subjects. It can also record 4K video at up to 30fps.
