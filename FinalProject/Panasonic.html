Panasonic Lumix GH6

The Panasonic Lumix GH6 is a high-end Micro Four Thirds mirrorless camera that is designed for professional videographers. It has a 25.2MP Live MOS sensor that can capture stunning images and videos in even the most challenging lighting conditions. It can also record 5.7K video at up to 30fps and C4K/4K UHD video at up to 60fps.
